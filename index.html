Hi, I'm Diogo! I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Copenhagen Center for Social Data Science (SODAS).

My research focuses on understanding and informing aspects of social and biological systems related to social behaviour, structure of social interactions, and mobility. I'm now developing new methodologies for comparing social interaction network data with theoretical models, applying them to both human and macaque social networks.
